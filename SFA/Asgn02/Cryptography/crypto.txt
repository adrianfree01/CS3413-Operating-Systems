Title: The Art of Problem Solving

Chapter 2: Analytical Problem Solving
In this chapter, we delve deeper into analytical problem-solving methods. We introduce strategies such as decomposition, pattern recognition, and algorithmic thinking. Chapter 3: Creative Problem Solving
Creativity is a crucial aspect of problem-solving. In this chapter, we explore ways to cultivate creativity and generate innovative solutions. Effective decision-making is integral to problem-solving. In this chapter, we discuss decision-making models, risk assessment techniques, and decision-making biases. By understanding these concepts, readers will make more informed decisions and enhance their problem-solving capabilities.

Chapter 5: Problem Solving in Real Life
Problem-solving skills are invaluable in various aspects of life, including personal, professional, and societal domains. Through hands-on activities and reflective exercises, readers will gain insights into applying problem-solving skills to navigate real-world challenges.
